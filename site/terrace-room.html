Terrace Room

Perched on the top floor with its own private terrace, this is the room for guests who want a little extra space and sky. Step through the glass doors onto your terrace for morning coffee with rooftop views over Cospicua's limestone skyline, or settle into the teal armchair with a book as the afternoon light filters in. Above the bed, a hand-painted Valletta sunset scene captures the golden glow that makes Malta's evenings unforgettable.

The Terrace Room is the most spacious in the house, with warm wood-effect floors, a full kitchenette with microwave and fridge, and a flat-screen TV for quiet evenings in. The ornate gold-framed mirror and elegant teal curtains bring a touch of boutique character to a room that already feels like your own private apartment in the Mediterranean. Breakfast is served each morning downstairs — but you might just want to take your coffee up to the terrace instead.
