Courtyard Room

The largest of our standard rooms at 29 square metres, the Courtyard Room is designed for guests who love to spread out. The generous layout includes a comfortable teal armchair for reading, a flat-screen TV, a full work desk, and a well-equipped kitchenette — all bathed in natural light from windows overlooking the building's quiet inner courtyard. Soft sage-green curtains and warm wood-effect floors create a calm, contemporary feel.

With its spacious proportions and thoughtful amenities, this room works beautifully for longer stays and guests who appreciate having room to breathe. The kitchenette area with coffee machine, microwave, and fridge flows naturally alongside the desk space, making it easy to work, cook a light meal, or simply enjoy a quiet morning before heading to breakfast. Available across three floors, each Courtyard Room offers a peaceful retreat from the sun-soaked streets outside.
