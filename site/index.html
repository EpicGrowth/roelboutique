Where Malta Feels Like Home

Nestled in the honey-gold limestone streets of Cospicua, Roel Boutique B&B is your invitation to experience Malta the way it's meant to be felt — personally, warmly, and authentically. Our lovingly restored heritage property blends original Maltese character with modern comforts, and our host Ledia is here to make sure your stay feels less like a hotel visit and more like coming home to a friend's beautiful house in the Mediterranean.

Rated 9.9 out of 10 on Booking.com, Roel is one of Malta's highest-rated accommodations — and we believe that's because we treat every guest as a person, not a room number.

Our Neighbourhood

Malta's Best-Kept Secret

While most visitors flock to Valletta and Sliema, the Three Cities — Cospicua, Birgu, and Senglea — are where Malta's real story unfolds. Quiet waterfront promenades, centuries of history around every corner, and the authentic rhythm of Maltese daily life. Step outside Roel and you're in the heart of it.

Learn More
Perfectly Connected

Ten Minutes to the Capital

A scenic ferry ride across the Grand Harbour — just ten minutes — and you're in Valletta, Malta's UNESCO World Heritage capital. St John's Co-Cathedral, Upper Barrakka Gardens, and world-class restaurants are all within walking distance of the ferry terminal. Explore the capital by day, come home to Roel's peaceful streets at night.
