Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about your winter stay? Find quick answers to our most frequently asked questions to make your visit seamless and enjoyable.

  • What are the check-in and check-out times?
    Check-in starts in the afternoon, and check-out is in the morning. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request.
  • Is ski equipment rental available at the hotel?
    Yes, ski equipment rental services are available nearby or within the resort for your convenience.
  • Do you offer ski passes or packages?
    We offer seasonal ski passes and special winter packages that can be arranged during your stay.
  • Is the hotel suitable for families with children?
    Absolutely. Our resort offers family-friendly accommodations and activities suitable for all ages.
  • Are spa and wellness services available?
    Yes, our spa and wellness facilities are available for guests looking to relax after a day on the slopes.
  • Is transportation to the ski slopes provided?
    Shuttle services to nearby ski slopes are available during the winter season.
  • What dining options are available at the hotel?
    Our hotel features restaurants and lounges offering local and international cuisine throughout the day.
  • Can I cancel or modify my reservation?
    Cancellation and modification policies vary depending on your booking. Please review your reservation details or contact us for assistance.
