Welcome
Welcome to Roel

Nestled in the honey-gold limestone streets of Cospicua, Roel Boutique B&B is your invitation to experience Malta the way it's meant to be felt — personally, warmly, and authentically. Our lovingly restored heritage property blends original Maltese character with modern comforts, and our host Ledia is here to make sure your stay feels less like a hotel visit and more like coming home to a friend's beautiful house in the Mediterranean.

Rated 9.9 out of 10 on Booking.com, Roel is one of Malta's highest-rated accommodations — and we believe that's because we treat every guest as a person, not a room number.
