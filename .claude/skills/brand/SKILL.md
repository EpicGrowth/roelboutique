# /brand — Roel Boutique B&B Brand Reference

## Identity

- **Name**: Roel Boutique B&B
- **Location**: Cospicua (Bormla), Three Cities, Malta
- **Type**: Heritage boutique bed & breakfast, 3-6 rooms
- **Host**: Ledia
- **Rating**: 9.9/10 on Booking.com
- **Tagline**: "Where Malta Feels Like Home"

## Brand Voice

- **Warm and personal** — "your host Ledia", not "hotel management"
- **Confident but not boastful** — Let the 9.9 rating speak; don't oversell
- **Locally rooted** — Three Cities insider, not generic Malta tourism
- **Heritage-forward** — Limestone walls, centuries of history, authentic character
- **Inviting** — "Come home to a friend's house in the Mediterranean"

### Tone Words
Authentic, personal, heritage, warm, boutique, Mediterranean, intimate, curated

### Avoid
Generic hotel language, corporate speak, "luxury" (use "boutique" instead), mass tourism vibes

## Key Differentiators

1. **Personal hospitality** — Ledia personally welcomes every guest
2. **Heritage property** — Real centuries-old limestone, not styled-to-look-old
3. **Three Cities location** — Authentic Malta, not tourist Sliema/St Julian's
4. **Breakfast included** — Freshly prepared with local Maltese ingredients
5. **Gateway position** — 5 min ferry to Valletta, 20 min to airport
