Welcome to Roel

Nestled in the honey-gold limestone streets of Cospicua, Roel Boutique B&B is your invitation to experience Malta the way it's meant to be felt — personally, warmly, and authentically. Our lovingly restored heritage property blends original Maltese character with modern comforts.

Rated 9.9 out of 10 on Booking.com, Roel is one of Malta's highest-rated accommodations — because we treat every guest as a person, not a room number. Host Ledia is here to make your stay feel like coming home to a friend's beautiful house in the Mediterranean.

The Three Cities

While most visitors flock to Valletta and Sliema, the Three Cities — Cospicua, Birgu, and Senglea — are Malta's best-kept secret. Quiet waterfront promenades, centuries of history, and the authentic rhythm of Maltese daily life.
